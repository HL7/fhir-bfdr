Resource Profile: Condition - Gestational Hypertension

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/bfdr/StructureDefinition/Condition-gestational-hypertension Version: 2.0.0-Ballot
Draft as of 2024-03-05 Computable Name: ConditionGestationalHypertension
